# Terms of Service — History After Dark Publisher

**Last updated: 21 July 2026**

## 1. What this is

History After Dark Publisher ("the App") is a personal software tool that
generates short-form video content and publishes it to a single TikTok
account operated by the App's developer.

## 2. Who may use it

The App is not offered to the public. It has one user — its developer,
who is also the owner of the connected TikTok account. There is no
sign-up, no user accounts, and no service provided to third parties.

## 3. Connected accounts

The App connects to TikTok only through TikTok's official OAuth flow and
Content Posting API, and only to the account that has explicitly
authorized it. Authorization can be withdrawn at any time from TikTok's
"Manage app permissions" page, which immediately ends the App's access.

## 4. Content

All content published through the App is created by its developer using
AI generation tools, and is submitted to TikTok flagged as AI-generated.
The developer is solely responsible for that content and for its
compliance with TikTok's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

## 5. Data

The App stores OAuth tokens and the connected account's identifier
locally on the developer's own machine. It operates no server and shares
no data with third parties. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.html) for
full detail.

The App is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. It is personal
software, not a commercial product.
