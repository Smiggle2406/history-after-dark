History After Dark Publisher

A personal content-publishing tool for a single TikTok account.

What it does

History After Dark Publisher assembles short-form historical education

videos and publishes them to the TikTok account operated by its

developer. The whole pipeline runs locally:

  • Narration scripts are written by a locally-hosted language model
  • Illustrations are generated locally from those scripts
  • Captions are timed by local speech recognition
  • Video is assembled locally with FFmpeg
  • Finished videos are published through TikTok's Content Posting API

Scope of use

This is a single-user tool. It has one authorized account — the

developer's own — and is not distributed publicly or offered as a service

to other creators. It does not access, read, or store data belonging to

any other TikTok user.

AI-generated content

All video content produced by this tool is AI-generated. Every post is

submitted with TikTok's is_aigc flag set, so it carries the "Creator

labeled as AI-generated" label.

Content

Videos cover documented historical events, figures, and mysteries. All

content is original, historically accurate, and written to be

platform-safe: no graphic material and no glorification of violence.
